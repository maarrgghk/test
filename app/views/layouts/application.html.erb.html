Superfight!

Players draw three white cards and three black cards. Each player chooses one white card and one black card from their hand to create a fighter and disgards the remaining cards. Each player then draws a random black card and adds it to their fighter.

Players must then argue and plead their cases about why their fighters would win the fight.
